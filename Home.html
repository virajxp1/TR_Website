What is Texas Raas?

Who we be?

Established in 2003, Texas Raas is a competitive raas/garba team at the University of Texas at Austin. Raas and garba are forms of energetic and playful dancing that originate from the state of Gujarat in India. Our co-ed team is generally comprised of 20-24 students of all ages.

More than just a dance team, Texas Raas is a family. Although we spend an inordinate amount of time with our teammates throughout the year, we never get bored of each other. Travelling to cool cities with your best friends, hanging out late nights after practice, and competing with many other teams nationally are only a handful of things that make the team so fun.

We also have a large and strong network of alumni that we are closely connected to that continues to grow every year!
